Natasha Carr

Hello reader! My name is Natasha AKA Tash. I live in Buckinghamshire in Milton Keynes, recognisable to most as 'that place with all the roundabouts'. I am a cat-person with no cats (yet!) and I recently got engaged to my partner of six wonderful years.

I've worked for almost a decade in retail operations, but web design has always held my interest. After a Covid-driven redundancy, I made the decision to plunge head-first into making waves to break into web design as my next career. I've been studying with The Open University for two years now, working towards a BSc (Honours) degree in Computing and IT, and will soon be specialising in a web design route. I've also completed a handful of Codecademy courses in various languages, as well as completed the Microsoft MTA: Intro to Programming with HTML and CSS exam.


I hold a Foundation Degree in Graphic Design and have had work from this recognised in a few settings, such as being used to theme an award ceremony for MK College, having a book cover design published, and having some of my work displayed at Centre:MK for an arts show.

Whilst most of my time is spent studying, working, or coding, I still find time here and there for other outlets. Gaming, reading, and Marvel movies are all things I enjoy, and I also like creating things with paper using a technique called quilling.
