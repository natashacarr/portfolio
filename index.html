Natasha Carr

Hello reader! My name is Natasha AKA Tash. I live in Buckinghamshire, in Milton Keynes, which is often known only as 'that place with all the roundabouts' - 124 and counting, I believe. I am a cat-person with no cats (yet!) and I recently got engaged to my partner of six wonderful years.

I've worked for almost a decade in retail operations, but web design has always held my interest. After a covid-based redundancy, I made the decision to plunge head-first into making waves to break into web design as my next career. I've been studying with The Open University for two years now, working towards a BSc (Honours) degree in Computing and IT, and will soon be specialising in a web design route. I've also completed a handful of Codecademy courses in various languages:

And completed the Microsoft MTA: Intro to Programming with HTML and CSS exam:

I also hold a Foundation Degree in Graphic Design and have had my work from this recognised in a few settings, such as being used to theme an award ceremony for MK College, having a book cover design published, and having some of my work displayed at Centre:MK for an arts show.

Whilst it is true that most of my time now is spent either studying, working, or coding, I still find a little time here and there for other outlets. Gaming, reading, and Marvel movies are all of interest to me, but I also try to create things in different ways too. During my graphics study, I came across a little-known craft called quilling - the art of manipulating thin strips of paper into beautiful works.
